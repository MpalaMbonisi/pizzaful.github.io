Pizzaful

In the heart of Naples, there was a small pizza place called Pizzaful. It was run by a family who had been making pizza for generations. Their secret was the homemade dough, which was crispy and light, with just the right amount of chewiness. The toppings were fresh and flavorful, from the sweet San Marzano tomatoes to the creamy mozzarella cheese. Pizzaful had a cozy atmosphere, with red and white checkered tablecloths and photos of famous Italian chefs on the walls.
